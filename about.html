Crafted for the Visionary

At eNVision, we believe that exceptional eyewear should empower your unique perspective. Every frame is meticulously designed to merge striking modern aesthetics with absolute optical precision, crafted specifically for those who look ahead
The eNVision story began in a modest optical workshop, founded by passionate opticians dedicated to combining modern design with precision eyewear. Over the years, this journey transformed us into a globally recognized store, attracting customers worldwide through our impeccable craftsmanship and flawless service.

"Arthur spent over fifteen years working as a master optician before establishing eNVision. His deep obsession with optical precision and classic eyewear architecture is what drives our commitment to creating the finest lenses and timeless frame designs today."

Arthur Vance, Co-Founder

"Bringing a modern aesthetic approach to the brand, Eleanor believes that every pair of glasses should be a celebration of modern design and finest materials. Her passion ensures that eNVision frames are not only functional but also serve as an elegant extension of personal style."

Eleanor Sterling, Co-Founder & Head of Design
